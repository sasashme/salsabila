Internship (DIPLOMA)

I interned at Pejabat Daerah dan Tanah Batang Padang, where I was part of the Rural Development Unit. This internship was a great opportunity to grow professionally while making lasting memories with my team ♥

Housekeeping (ILF)

During my SPM years, I worked as a housekeeper at the Felda Training Institute Hotel in Trolak, Sungkai, Perak. This valuable experience taught me attention to detail, teamwork, and hospitality. The memories of working in a dynamic environment with a supportive team inspire me to this day.
